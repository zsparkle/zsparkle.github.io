Join Us for Z'Sparkle Drag Race 2014!

JOIN US IN THE FIGHT AGAINST ALZHEIMER’S
Z’SPARKLE PARTY AND DRAG RACE TO END ALZHEIMERS
FRIDAY AUGUST 8th
JOIN US AS A “DRAGSTAR”
WE’D LOVE TO HAVE YOU!

WILL YOU STEP UP TO THE CHALLENGE?
WILL YOU HELP US HELP PUT AND END TO ALZHEIMER’S DISEASE?

What it is:

The event is a fundraiser in conjunction with our Alzheimer’s Association Walk to End Alzheimer’s Team, Lacy’s Lights. We are raising funds and awareness for Alzheimer’s disease support and research. All money raised will go to the Alzheimer's Association Greater Indiana Chapter.

Why Z’Sparkle?:

In the amount of time it takes an average sparkler to burn, 68 seconds, another person will be diagnosed with Alzheimer’s Disease. Z’Sparkle recognizes this fact and honors and remembers the sparkle the victims of Alzheimer's once had, the sparkle in their eyes; the sparkle of their love and talents and spirit and the sparkle that their lives brought to all those whose lives they touched. The Z’Sparkle event also celebrates the spark of hope that someday we'll find a cure to this devastating disease which currently affects 5.4 million Americans and is the 6th leading cause of death in the U.S.

Gentlemen:

Your Mission, should you decide you are Man enough to don a dress and accept the challenge:
As a dragstar racer, you will be expected to dress in your finest dress – that’s right – dress! If it’s sparkly, even better. High heels are encouraged – and all the accessories.

To help you “suit up” for this race, you are asked to recruit a pit crew, one or two helpers to help you raise money and get ready to race down Main Street. How you raise the money will be up to you and your crew, you can do it before the event and the night of the event, during the party and the race. You can recruit sponsors before and during the event, sell autographs, sell trinkets, whatever! Be creative! You and your pit crew can go all out! Just keep it in good clean fun!

Ladies:

Your Mission, should you decide you are Wo-Man enough to donpants and accept the challenge:
As a "Dudette" racer, you will be expected to dress in your manliest garb! If it’s sparkly, even better. Facial hair is encouraged and whatever else it takes to help you “man up.”

To help you “suit up” for this race, you are asked to recruit a pit crew, one or two helpers to help you raise money and get ready to race down Main Street. How you raise the money will be up to you and your crew, you can do it before the event and the night of the event, during the party and the race. You can recruit sponsors before and during the event, sell autographs, sell trinkets, whatever! Be creative! You and your pit crew can go all out! Just keep it in good clean fun!

What to Expect on Z'Race Night if You Accept!

Pre-Race Dragstar Gathering:

6:00 PM: Pre-Race Dragster Gathering:
Beginning at 6:00 pm, dragstars, dudettes and their “crews” are invited to gather on Main Street for pre-race instructions, encouragement and a chance to partake of a little “starting fluid” of your choice - snacks will be provided.
Adults only please!

7:00 PM VIP TENT ON MAIN STREET
21 and over ONLY
The real party happens at the VIP tent in the old Shell parking lot at Pine and Main Street.
*Presale tickets are a $10.00 donation to the Alzheimer’s Association – available at Akard’s True Value and Zionsville Chamber of Commerce.
They admit the bearer to an evening of food, music and fun and a chance to mingle with you – the dragstars.
Admission for dragstars and up to three of their “pit crew” is free.
You will come to the tent immediately after the race and continue to party and fundraise in your own way.
This is an adults only event. There will be delicious hors d'oeuvres and sweets (provided by renowned Chef Matt Hybrich and his staff from Zionsville Meadows culinary services) as well as a cash bar, DJ, music and more but the real fun will be you!
The tent opens at 7:00pm and remains open after the race till 10:00 pm.

8:00 PM: The Race:* Please note NEW TIME!!
Each dragstar will be announced and after a “parade” down Main where you can collect donations in any (good, clean) way you see fit, theflag will drop and you “gentleman who are ladies” and “ladies who are gentlemen” will start your “engines” and race, waddle, hobble and crawl down Main Street in a Sparkly Parade of Splendor to the finish line!

NEW THIS YEAR!!!

There will be three “heats” for the race – Men (Dragstars) can choose to run “Flat” in running shoes –
or if you’re brave enough- you can choose to run “Stilleto” in your high heels.
Ladies (Dudettes) will run “Wing Tip” in your best men’s shoes. There will be a winner for each race

Please encourage your family and friends to purchase the pre-sale VIP tickets at
Akard’s True Value and the Chamber of Commerce - seats are limited!!!
*The entire price of the ticket goes to the Alzheimer’s Association and is a tax deductible donation.
Though the pre-race Z’Sparkle gathering is just for you and your “pit crew”, the Main Street race (and VIP tent) will be advertised and open to the public, so be prepared to be cheered and jeered!
And remember – this is ALL in good fun!
AND THE REAL WINNER IS –
THE VICTIMS OF ALZHEIMER’S, THEIR FAMILIES AND THE MILLIONS WHO ARE TOUCHED BY THIS DISEASE EVERY DAY.
