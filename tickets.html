VIP Tent Party

Opens at 7:00pm Friday August 8th

21 and over ONLY

Main & Pine Street

Tickets $10.00
Proceeds will go to Alzheimer's Association Greater Indiana Chapter

Seating is limited!

Advance tickets available at Akard's True Value Hardware or below (processing fees do apply).

Meet and mingle with "Dragstars" and “Dudettes”. Enjoy delicious food and a cash bar all while listening to the fabulous DJ rock out to our favorites.

Watch some of our community’s finest and bravest gentlemen adorned in their best dresses and high heels and our finest ladies duded up in their manliest gear, as they "race" down Main Street in a sparkly parade of splendor in exchange for donations to the Alzheimer's Association of Greater Indiana.

Then stay and watch the VIP party really heat up after the race!

Awards Presentation in the tent after the race to recognize most outstanding in several categories like: “Best Legs,” “Best Hair,” “Best Pit Crew,” “Best Dressed” and lots more!!

VIP Tent is for Over 21 only

*This event is rain or shine. No rain date. No Refunds. Ticket price is a donation to the Alzheimer’s Association*
