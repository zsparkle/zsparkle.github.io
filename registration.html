What to Expect on Z'Sparkle Drag Race Night?

6:00 PM: Pre-Race Dragster Gathering:

Beginning at 6:00 pm, dragstars, dudettes and their “crews” are invited to gather at the VIP Tent Main Street for pre-race instructions, encouragement and a chance to partake in a little “starting fluid” of your choice - snacks will be provided. Adults only please!

Must be 21 and over to participate as dragstar or dudette

7:00 PM: VIP TENT ON MAIN STREET

The real party happens at the VIP tent in Town Lot at Pine and Main Street. Presale tickets are a $10.00 donation to the Alzheimer’s Association – available at Akard’s True Value or online HERE for $10.00 + processing fees. They admit the bearer to an evening of food, music, fun, and a chance to mingle with you – the DRAGSTARS. Admission for dragstars and up to three of their “pit crew” is free. You will come to the tent immediately after the race and continue to party and fundraise in your own way. This is an ADULTS only event. There will be delicious hors d'oeuvres and sweets courtesy of Zionsville Meadows as well as a cash bar provided by Cobblestone Grill, DJ, and much more but the real fun will be you! The tent opens at 7:00pm and remains open after the race till 10:00 pm.

8:00 PM: The Race

Each dragstar will be announced and after a “parade” down Main Street where you can collect donations in any (good, clean) way you see fit, the flag will drop and you “gentleman who are ladies” and “ladies who are gentlemen” will start your “engines” and race, waddle, hobble, and crawl down Main Street in a Sparkly Parade of Splendor to the finish line!

There will be three “heats” for the race – Men (Dragstars) can choose to run “Flat” in running shoes – or if you’re brave enough- you can choose to run “Stiletto” in your high heels. Ladies (Dudettes) will run “Wing Tip” in your best men’s shoes. There will be a winner for each race.

Please encourage your family and friends to purchase the pre-sale VIP tickets at Akard’s True Value or online HERE – seating is limited!!! Proceeds from this event will go to the Alzheimer's Association Greater Indiana Chapter. Though the pre-race Z’Sparkle gathering is just for you and your “pit crew”, the Main Street race and VIP tent will be advertised and open to the public. Be prepared to be cheered and jeered! And remember – this is ALL in good fun!

9:00 PM: VIP Tent After Party

Food, Cash Bar, DJ, Music * Awards Presentation to recognize “Best Of” categories. Participants return to the VIP tent immediately after the race winners are "crowned" (approximately 9:00 pm) for the Awards Presentation and continue to party and fundraise for this worthy cause.

And the real winners are…the victims of Alzheimer’s, their families, and the millions who are touched by this disease every day.
